Activities

At Our Resort, we believe in offering more than just a place to stay; we provide a gateway to unforgettable experiences. From thrilling adventures to relaxing pastimes, our range of activities ensures that there's something for everyone to enjoy during their stay.

Precision Shooting with PCP Air Rifles

Step into the world of precision shooting with our PCP (Pre-Charged Pneumatic) air rifle shooting activity at Our Resort. Whether you're a seasoned marksman or trying your hand at shooting for the first time, this thrilling activity promises excitement and skill-building in a safe and controlled environment.

Billiards

Unwind and showcase your skills with a game of billiards at Our Resort's dedicated billiards lounge. Whether you're a seasoned player or looking to try your hand at this classic game, our well-appointed facilities and relaxed ambiance provide the perfect setting for an enjoyable and competitive experience.

Videoke

Experience the joy of singing and entertainment with our videoke (karaoke) activity at Our Resort's vibrant and lively karaoke lounge. Whether you're a seasoned performer or someone who loves to sing for fun, our state-of-the-art videoke system and extensive song library ensure a memorable and enjoyable karaoke experience for all.

Swimming

Embark on a refreshing and rejuvenating aquatic journey at Our Resort's stunning swimming pool. Nestled and designed for both relaxation and recreation, our swimming pool offers a perfect oasis for guests of all ages to unwind, play, and bask in the beauty of nature.
