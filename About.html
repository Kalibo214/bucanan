About

Welcome to Bucanan Farm Resort, where tranquility meets entertainment in a perfect blend of luxury and leisure.

The Bucanan Farmland was just a rest house used for planting and DIY projects. However, friends and relatives who often visited suggested,
"Why not open it to the public?" and so the rest house in Bucanan later became a resort that offers a peaceful
and calming overnight stay and became official in the year 2021.

Immerse yourself in the soothing ambiance of nature as you take a dip in our three inviting pools, one nestled serenely amidst towering trees, providing a refreshing oasis of calm. Your family will like our two spacious rooms, with one in front of the pool, designed to accommodate up to four adults. To complete your luxury experience, Bucanan Farm Resort also has two separate videoke rooms and an outdoor billiard pool. At Bucanan Farm Resort, every aspect of your stay is designed to elevate your experience, ensuring memories that will last a lifetime. Come, indulge in the tranquility and luxury of our sanctuary, where nature and leisure intertwine harmoniously.
